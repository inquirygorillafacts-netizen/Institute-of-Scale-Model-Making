Curriculum

Master Scale Model Making

India's first institute preparing students with AI and hands-on skills for top companies. Learn everything from scratch to advanced level.

Scale Models

Scale Model Making: Scratch to Adv.

Master the art of creating high-quality scale models from scratch. Learn material selection, precise cutting, assembling techniques, and professional finishing.

3D Design

3D Modeling & Design

Learn to design intricate parts and full models using industry-standard 3D software. Prepare your digital designs for 3D printing and CNC machining.

CNC & Fab

CNC Cutting & Machining

Gain hands-on experience in operating CNC routers and laser cutters. Learn to convert 2D/3D files into precise physical components for your models.

Software

Adv. Software Training

Deep dive into advanced CAD software for architectural and industrial models. Learn parametric modeling, rendering, and complex geometries.

Electronics

LED & Electrical Integration

Bring your models to life with dynamic lighting. Learn basic electronics, soldering, micro-controllers, and seamless LED integration techniques.

Advanced

Large-Scale Industrial Models

Specialize in planning, fabricating, and assembling massive industrial scale models. Learn structural integrity, teamwork, and project management.
